CANADA

Canada was a long awaited holiday in 2018. Starting point was Vancouver in western Canada, located in the Lower Mainland region of British Columbia.

Our roadtrip lead us through the Rocky Mountains, a major mountain range and the largest mountain system in North America, ending in Calgary. Calgary is the largest city in the western Canadian province of Alberta and is located in the transitional area between the Rocky Mountain Foothills and the Canadian Prairies, about 80 km (50 mi) east of the front ranges of the Canadian Rockies.
